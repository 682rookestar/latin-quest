## Review checklist

- Confirm supplier legal entity and service plan.
- Execute or accept Article 28-compliant terms before pupil processing.
- Record current subprocessors, purposes and locations.
- Ensure advance notice and objection process for new subprocessors.
- Verify deletion/return, breach notification, rights assistance and audit clauses.
- Determine whether each flow is a restricted transfer.
- Record the applicable adequacy regulation, DPF/data bridge, IDTA/Addendum or SCC.
- Complete the UK data protection test/transfer risk assessment where required.
- Check technical supplementary measures, government-access risks and onward transfers.
- Review annually and when supplier terms/subprocessors change.

## Important location statement

Selecting London for Supabase and Vercel compute reduces routine processing
distance but does not guarantee that all personal information stays in the UK.
Public notices and school contracts must not promise UK- or EU-only residency
unless an appropriate supplier product and verified architecture provide it.
