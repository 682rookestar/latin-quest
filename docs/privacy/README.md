# Latin Quest privacy and data-protection pack

Last reviewed: 22 July 2026

These documents provide an operational starting point for UK GDPR, the Data
Protection Act 2018, PECR and child-focused privacy compliance. They do not by
themselves certify compliance and should be approved by the operator and each
participating school's data-protection lead before production use.

## Documents

- `DPIA.md` — child-focused data protection impact assessment.
- `LIA.md` — legitimate interests assessment for processing where Latin Quest
  acts as controller.
- `RECORD_OF_PROCESSING.md` — data map, purposes, recipients and safeguards.
- `RETENTION_AND_DELETION.md` — retention schedule and deletion procedure.
- `RIGHTS_AND_BREACHES.md` — subject-rights and incident-response procedures.
- `VENDORS_AND_TRANSFERS.md` — processor and international-transfer register.
- `SCHOOL_DPA_TEMPLATE.md` — controller-to-processor terms for legal review.
- `CHILDRENS_CODE.md` — assessment against the Children's Code standards.

## Approval blockers

The following facts must be completed before these documents are signed off:

1. Monitored privacy email and escalation contact.
2. Operator's exact legal identity and business/contact address.
3. Whether Latin Quest is operated personally, by a company, or by a school.
4. Archived evidence of the Vercel Pro subscription and the applicable DPA;
   Pro status was confirmed by the account owner on 22 July 2026.
5. Supabase DPA acceptance and current subprocessor list review.
6. School contract model and the controller/processor allocation for each use.
7. Agreed contract-end and inactivity retention periods.
8. Confirmation of whether users in the EEA are intentionally served.
9. Confirmation that no optional analytics or marketing integrations are
   enabled in the deployed Vercel project.

## Release gate

Do not make an unqualified statement that Latin Quest “is GDPR compliant”. A
responsible release statement is that it has been designed to support UK GDPR
compliance, subject to the participating school's responsibilities and the
documented contractual and operational controls.
